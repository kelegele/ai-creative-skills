# Design System: Agentic

> Source: [Open Design — Agentic](https://open-design.ai/zh/plugins/systems/agentic)
> Category: Themed & Unique
> Conversational AI-first interface with minimal controls, clear outcomes, and delegated task flows for agentic workflows.

## Color

- **Primary:** `#FF5701` — CTA emphasis, headlines accent
- **Secondary:** `#F6F6F1` — subtle surface backgrounds
- **Success:** `#16A34A`
- **Warning:** `#D97706`
- **Danger:** `#DC2626`
- **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` — large backgrounds and cards
- **Text:** `#111827` — body copy
- **Neutral:** `#FFFFFF`

Rules:
- Favor Primary (#FF5701) for CTA emphasis.
- Use Surface (#FFFFFF) for large backgrounds and cards.
- Keep body copy on Text (#111827) for legibility.

## Typography

- **Scale:** 14 / 16 / 18 / 24 / 32 / 40
- **Families:** primary=Playfair Display, display=Playfair Display, mono=JetBrains Mono
- **Weights:** 100–900 (full range)
- Headings carry style personality; body text optimizes scanability and contrast.

## Spacing & Grid

- 8pt baseline grid
- Keep vertical rhythm consistent across sections.
- Align columns to predictable grid.

## Layout & Composition

- Clear content blocks with consistent internal padding.
- Hierarchy: headline → support text → primary action.
- Use whitespace to separate concerns before adding borders or shadows.

## Components

- Buttons: primary action uses `#FF5701`; secondary actions stay neutral.
- Cards/sections: consistent radii, spacing, and elevation.

## Motion

- Subtle transitions emphasizing Primary (#FF5701).
- Short, purposeful transitions (150–250ms).

## Voice & Brand

- Concise, confident, product-specific.
- Action-oriented microcopy.

## Anti-patterns

- No off-palette colors when existing token works.
- No flattening hierarchy with same size/weight everywhere.
- No decorative effects reducing readability.
- No mixing unrelated visual metaphors.
